Sandy Salmon Bed & Breakfast Lodge

(503) 622-6699

Whether you are looking for a relaxing hideaway, an exciting place to play, or just passing through, we can accommodate your desires.
The lodge sits on a bluff about 60 feet above the Sandy River and the Salmon River where they merge in a maze of rapids and whitewater.
As you approach the lodge entrance your eye is taken to the double door entry with bronze door pulls of jumping salmon, designed and created by a local artist.
